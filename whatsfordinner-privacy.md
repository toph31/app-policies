# What's For Dinner Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## Overview

What's For Dinner is a meal planning app that helps you decide what to eat.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Meal preferences and favorites
- Custom recipes you add
- Grocery list items
- Calendar meal plans

### Automatically Collected
- Basic usage analytics (anonymized)
- Crash reports

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide meal suggestions
- To save your preferences
- To sync across your devices (optional)
- To improve the app

## Data Storage

- Data is stored locally on your device
- Optional iCloud sync is encrypted
- We do not store your data on our servers

## Third-Party Services

- Recipe APIs for meal suggestions
- Apple's iCloud for optional sync

## Advertising

This app may display ads. Our ad partners may collect anonymized device identifiers. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

## Your Rights

- Delete your data anytime within the app
- Disable cloud sync
- Opt out of analytics
