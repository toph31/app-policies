# RemotePro Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## Overview

RemotePro is a universal TV remote control app.

## Information We Collect

### Device Discovery
- The app scans your local network to find compatible TVs and devices
- This data stays on your device and is not transmitted externally

### Automatically Collected
- Basic usage analytics (anonymized)
- Crash reports

## Permissions

- **Local Network:** To discover and control TVs on your WiFi
- **Bluetooth:** For devices that use Bluetooth control

## Data Storage

- Device configurations stored locally
- No cloud account required
- No personal data leaves your device

## Third-Party Services

- None for core functionality

## Your Rights

- All data is local
- Delete the app to remove all data
