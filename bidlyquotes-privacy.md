# BidlyQuotes Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## Overview

BidlyQuotes is a quoting and estimating platform for contractors.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Name and email address
- Business name and contact details
- Payment information (processed securely by Stripe)

### Business Data
- Quotes and estimates you create
- Customer information you enter
- Project details and pricing

### Automatically Collected
- Usage analytics
- Device and browser information
- Error logs

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide the quoting service
- To process payments
- To send important account notifications
- To improve our platform
- To provide customer support

## Data Storage & Security

- Data is stored on secure cloud servers
- All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS)
- We use industry-standard security practices
- Regular backups to prevent data loss

## Third-Party Services

- Stripe for payment processing
- Cloud hosting providers
- Email service providers

## Your Rights

- Export your data at any time
- Delete your account and all data
- Access and update your information
- Opt out of marketing emails

## Data Retention

- Active account data is retained while you use the service
- Deleted accounts are purged within 30 days
- Some data may be retained for legal/tax purposes
