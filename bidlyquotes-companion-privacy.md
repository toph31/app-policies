# BidlyQuotes Companion Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## Overview

BidlyQuotes Companion is the mobile app for BidlyQuotes quoting platform.

## Information We Collect

This app connects to your BidlyQuotes account. See our main privacy policy at bidlyquotes.com for complete details.

### Mobile-Specific Data
- Device information for push notifications
- Camera access (for document scanning, with permission)
- Location (optional, for job site tracking)

## How We Use Your Information

- To sync with your BidlyQuotes account
- To send push notifications about quotes
- To enable document scanning features

## Permissions

- **Camera:** To scan documents and receipts
- **Notifications:** To alert you about quote activity
- **Location:** Optional, for job site features

## Data Storage

- Data syncs with BidlyQuotes cloud servers
- Local cache for offline access
- Encrypted storage on device
