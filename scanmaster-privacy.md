# ScanMaster Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## Overview

ScanMaster is a QR code and barcode scanner app.

## Information We Collect

### Camera Access
- Camera is used only to scan codes
- Images are processed locally and not stored or transmitted

### Scan History
- Optional scan history stored locally on your device
- You can disable or clear history anytime

### Automatically Collected
- Basic usage analytics (anonymized)
- Crash reports

## Permissions

- **Camera:** Required to scan codes

## Data Storage

- All data stored locally on device
- No account required
- No cloud sync

## Third-Party Services

- None for core functionality

## Your Rights

- Clear scan history anytime
- Camera permission can be revoked in Settings
- Delete the app to remove all data
