# StitchLogic Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## Overview

StitchLogic ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Quilting projects and patterns you create
- Fabric inventory data you enter
- Account information (if you create an account)

### Automatically Collected Information
- Basic device information (device type, OS version)
- App usage analytics (features used, crash reports)

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and improve the app's functionality
- To sync your data across devices (if enabled)
- To fix bugs and improve performance

## Data Storage

- Your quilting projects are stored locally on your device
- If you enable cloud sync, data is stored securely in iCloud
- We do not sell your personal data to third parties

## Third-Party Services

The app may use:
- Apple's iCloud for data synchronization
- Analytics services to improve app performance

## Your Rights

You can:
- Export your data at any time
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Opt out of analytics in app settings

## Children's Privacy

StitchLogic does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
