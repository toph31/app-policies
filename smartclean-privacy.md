# SmartClean Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## Overview

SmartClean is a photo cleaning utility that helps you manage storage on your device.

## Information We Collect

### Photos Analysis
- SmartClean analyzes photos **locally on your device**
- Photos are **never uploaded** to any server
- All duplicate detection happens on-device

### Analytics
- Basic usage statistics (anonymized)
- Crash reports

## Permissions

SmartClean requires photo library access to:
- Scan for duplicate photos
- Identify similar images
- Calculate storage usage

**We never access your photos for any other purpose.**

## Data Storage

- No photos leave your device
- No personal data is stored on our servers
- Settings are stored locally

## Third-Party Services

- Apple's Photos framework (local only)
- Optional: anonymized crash reporting

## Your Rights

- Revoke photo access anytime in Settings
- All analysis is local — nothing to delete from servers
