## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: How do I request a refund?**
A: Refunds are handled by Apple. Go to [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com) to request a refund.

**Q: How do I restore my purchases?**
A: In the app, go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.

**Q: The app crashed. What should I do?**
A: Try closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists, try deleting and reinstalling the app. Your purchases will be restored.

### Privacy

**Q: Do you collect my data?**
A: No. All our apps process data locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or share your personal information.
