# Always On Alarm Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## Overview

Always On Alarm is a reliable alarm clock app.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Alarm times and settings
- Custom alarm labels

### Automatically Collected
- Basic usage analytics (anonymized)
- Crash reports

## Data Storage

- All alarm data is stored locally on your device
- No personal data is sent to our servers
- No account required

## Permissions

- **Notifications:** Required for alarm alerts
- **Background App Refresh:** To ensure alarms fire reliably

## Third-Party Services

None. This app works entirely offline.

## Your Rights

- All data is on your device
- Delete the app to remove all data
