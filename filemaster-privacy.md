# FileMaster Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## Overview

FileMaster is a file management app for organizing your documents.

## Information We Collect

### File Access
- The app accesses files you choose to manage
- Files remain on your device or your cloud accounts
- We do not access, read, or transmit your file contents

### Automatically Collected
- Basic usage analytics (anonymized)
- Crash reports

## Permissions

- **Files & Folders:** To manage and organize your documents

## Data Storage

- App preferences stored locally
- Your files remain in their original locations
- No data transmitted to our servers

## Third-Party Services

- Your cloud storage providers (iCloud, Dropbox, etc.) if you connect them

## Your Rights

- Full control over which files the app can access
- Revoke access anytime
- Delete the app to remove app data
