# DocScanner Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## Overview

DocScanner helps you scan documents and create PDFs.

## Information We Collect

### Camera & Photos
- Camera is used to capture document images
- Photos are processed locally for scanning
- Documents are stored only on your device or your chosen cloud service

### Automatically Collected
- Basic usage analytics (anonymized)
- Crash reports

## Permissions

- **Camera:** To scan documents
- **Photos:** To access existing images for scanning
- **Files:** To save PDF documents

## Data Storage

- All scanned documents stored locally
- Optional export to your iCloud, Dropbox, etc. (your accounts)
- We do not store your documents on our servers

## Third-Party Services

- Your cloud storage provider (if you choose to export)

## Your Rights

- All documents remain on your device
- Export or delete documents anytime
- Revoke camera access in Settings
