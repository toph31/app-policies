# Kids Eat Free Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## Overview

Kids Eat Free helps families find restaurants where kids eat free.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- With your permission, we access your location to find nearby deals
- Location is used only for search and is not stored on our servers

### Information You Provide
- Saved favorite restaurants
- Deal submissions (if you report a deal)

### Automatically Collected
- Basic usage analytics (anonymized)
- Crash reports

## How We Use Your Information

- To show nearby kids-eat-free deals
- To improve deal accuracy
- To enhance app features

## Data Storage

- Favorites are stored locally on your device
- Location data is processed in real-time, not stored
- Deal database is on secure cloud servers

## Third-Party Services

- Apple Maps for directions
- Location services (with your permission)

## Advertising

This app may display ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in device settings.

## Children's Privacy

This app is designed for parents/guardians. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Your Rights

- Disable location access anytime
- Delete saved favorites
- Opt out of analytics
