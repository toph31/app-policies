# VoiceNote Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## Overview

VoiceNote is a voice recording and memo app.

## Information We Collect

### Audio Recordings
- Recordings are stored locally on your device
- We do not access, listen to, or transmit your recordings
- Optional cloud backup to your iCloud

### Automatically Collected
- Basic usage analytics (anonymized)
- Crash reports

## Permissions

- **Microphone:** Required to record audio

## Data Storage

- All recordings stored locally on device
- Optional iCloud sync (your account, encrypted)
- We do not store recordings on our servers

## Third-Party Services

- Apple's iCloud for optional sync/backup

## Your Rights

- Full control over your recordings
- Delete recordings anytime
- Revoke microphone access in Settings
- Export recordings in standard formats
