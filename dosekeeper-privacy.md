# DoseKeeper Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## Overview

DoseKeeper ("we", "our", or "the app") is a medication tracking application committed to protecting your health information and privacy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Medication names, dosages, and schedules
- Health notes and symptoms (if entered)
- Emergency contact information (optional)

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device information (device type, OS version)
- App usage analytics (anonymized)
- Crash reports

## How We Use Your Information

- To send medication reminders
- To track your medication history
- To improve app functionality
- We **never** sell your health data

## Data Storage & Security

- All health data is stored locally on your device
- iCloud sync is optional and encrypted
- We use industry-standard security practices
- Your data is never stored on our servers

## HIPAA Notice

DoseKeeper is designed for personal use and is not a covered entity under HIPAA. However, we treat your health information with the same level of care.

## Third-Party Services

The app may use:
- Apple HealthKit (with your permission)
- Apple's iCloud for optional data sync
- RxNorm API for medication information (no personal data sent)

## Your Rights

You can:
- Export all your medication data
- Delete all data from the app
- Disable cloud sync at any time
- Opt out of analytics

## Children's Privacy

DoseKeeper is not intended for use by children under 13 without parental supervision.

## Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
