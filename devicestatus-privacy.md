# DeviceStatus Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## Overview

DeviceStatus monitors your device's battery, storage, and system health.

## Information We Collect

### Device Information
- Battery level and health
- Storage usage
- Memory usage
- System information

This information is displayed to you and not transmitted anywhere.

### Automatically Collected
- Basic usage analytics (anonymized)
- Crash reports

## Data Storage

- All monitoring data stays on your device
- No account required
- No data sent to external servers

## Permissions

No special permissions required.

## Third-Party Services

None for core functionality.

## Your Rights

- All data is local
- Delete the app to remove all data
