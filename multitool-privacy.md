# MultiTool Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## Overview

MultiTool is a utility app with flashlight, compass, level, and other tools.

## Information We Collect

### Sensor Access
- Compass uses magnetometer (processed locally)
- Level uses accelerometer (processed locally)
- No sensor data is stored or transmitted

### Automatically Collected
- Basic usage analytics (anonymized)
- Crash reports

## Permissions

- **Camera (Flashlight):** To control the flash LED
- **Location (Compass):** For accurate compass calibration

## Data Storage

- No personal data is stored
- No account required
- All functionality is local

## Third-Party Services

None.

## Your Rights

- Revoke permissions anytime in Settings
- Delete the app to remove all data
